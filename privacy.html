Privacy

We don't collect anything personal.

Be Your Own Broker is built the way a tool that's actually on your side should be. Here's the whole policy in plain English.

The tools run in your browser

The Finance-Office Check does all its math locally, on your own device. The numbers you type — prices, rates, payments — are never sent to us or anyone else. Close the tab and they're gone.

No accounts, no ad tracking

There's nothing to sign up for. We don't ask for your name, email, or phone number. We don't run advertising cookies, behavioral tracking, session recording, or cross-site profiling, and we don't sell or share data.

Privacy-friendly analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to see basic, aggregate traffic — roughly how many people visit and which pages they read. It's cookieless: it sets no cookies, doesn't fingerprint your device, and doesn't follow you across other sites or build a profile of you. It collects no information that identifies you personally. That's also why there's no cookie-consent banner here — there's nothing to consent to.

If that ever changes

If we later add something that collects more — say, an advertising slot — we'll say so here first, explain exactly what it does, and give you a clear way to opt out. This page is the honest record; we update it before, not after.
